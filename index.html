Small Business Loan Eligibility Expert System

Use Case Description

This expert system helps small business owners in Rwanda and Africa determine their eligibility for loans based on key factors like revenue, credit score, and debt-to-income ratio. By providing quick and transparent assessments, the system promotes financial inclusion and supports the growth of small businesses.

Key Stakeholders

  • Small Business Owners (Primary Users)
  • Banks and Microfinance Institutions
  • Government and Regulatory Bodies
  • Developers and System Administrators

IF-THEN Rules

  • IF monthly revenue ≥ 500,000 RWF THEN eligible for loan
  • IF business age ≥ 2 years THEN eligible for loan
  • IF credit score ≥ 70 THEN eligible for loan
  • IF debt-to-income ratio ≤ 40% THEN eligible for loan
  • IF collateral is provided THEN increase loan limit
  • IF no previous defaults THEN eligible for higher amounts
